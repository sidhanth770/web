About Us



COVID-19 has showed the world that investing time, money & innovation into the health sector is very important. In Pakistan, we have been facing issues with accessibility to medical supplies in these hard times. Shifa is a marketplace that addresses this problem through e-commerce.


We provide accessibility and affordability for medical instruments & medication along with medical advice through chatbot for people looking for eHealth consultation. We envision that through this platform healthcare will be accessible to all from the comfort of their homes.
